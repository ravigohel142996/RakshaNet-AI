# Pilot Business Model

## Revenue Streams
- One-time hardware installation fee.
- Monthly monitoring SaaS subscription.
- Annual maintenance and support contract (AMC).

## Go-to-Market Channels
- NGOs and women safety programs.
- Educational institutions and hostels.
- Local government/rural development bodies.
- Factory and logistics corridor operators.

## Pilot Packaging
- 10-pole starter cluster + 200 wearables.
- Dashboard access + escalation playbook.
- SLA-backed support for safety incidents.
